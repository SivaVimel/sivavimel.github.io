Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Car Run ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the application. Car Run is published by SVR and is available on Google Play under the package name com.svr.carrun.

We designed Car Run to collect as little information as possible. The App itself does not require an account, does not ask for personal details, and does not track your identity.

Information We Collect

Information collected by the App

Car Run stores the following data locally on your device only using Android's PlayerPrefs system:

  • Best score and best distance
  • Selected car preference
  • Music and sound effect volume settings

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the App.

Information collected by advertising partners

Car Run displays banner advertisements during gameplay using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process the following to serve and measure ads:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device identifiers and general device information
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected directly by Google and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

How Information Is Used

  • To save your in-game progress and preferences locally on your device
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal information with third parties beyond the advertising network described above.

Permissions

Car Run requests only the INTERNET permission, which is required to load advertisements from Google AdMob. No other Android permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

Car Run is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertisements served by AdMob are configured to comply with Google Play's Families Policy where applicable.

Your Choices

Reset your Advertising ID

You can reset or delete your Advertising ID at any time from Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Clear local data

You can clear all locally stored progress by uninstalling the App or clearing its data from Android Settings → Apps → Car Run → Storage.

Third-Party Services

Car Run integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — Privacy Policy
